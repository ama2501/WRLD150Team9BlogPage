Inter/Cultural Events and Activities Online

Enrich Your Cultural Journey Virtually

Accessing intercultural events online is not only convenient, but it allows you to join from anywhere in the world, saving travel time. Attending these events expands your cultural knowledge through diverse audiences. Virtual networking facilitates connections with participants, leading to meaningful exchanges and increased cultural understanding.

In this blog page, we have collected information on cultural events and activities that you could participate in, which are accessible online!

Here are our top recommendations:


Online Radio for Music All Over the World

Online radio allows us to access music channels from all over the world anytime and anywhere. At Accu Radio, you can freely choose music channels from different countries and with different themes. Here we can find the call from Africa, authentic Japanese enka, and Spanish flamenco... No registration is required! At Hit Radio, There is also no need to download, you can just click play to get random hit songs from all over the world.

  • Location: Online
  • Price: Free
  • Date: Anytime

Eating Up Easter

Join for a screening of Sergio Mata’u Rapu and Elena Kouneski Rapu film documentary Eating up Easter. Set in Rapa Nui (Easter Island, Chile), this film explores the conflict between indigenous populations and local communities as they both work towards economic development and environmental sustainability. Don't miss this thought-provoking documentary that displays the delicate balance between progress and preservation in one of the world's most remote and beautiful places!

  • Location: Online
  • Price: Donation
  • Date: June 24, 2024 5:00pm-7:00pm PDT
